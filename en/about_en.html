About Plaza Network

Our mission

Important

Plaza Network is an unofficial fan-created site and is in no way affiliated, associated, authorized or endorsed by the developers and owners of Neocron. This site is an independent initiative of fans dedicated to the game.

Plaza Network was created with a simple aim: to gather and share detailed information about the Neocron universe, to help players better understand and enjoy this unique cyberpunk MMORPG.
