Neocron's factions

City Administration

The authoritarian government that controls Neocron with an iron fist under the leadership of President Reza.
They impose order and law, regarding rebels as terrorists. Backed by powerful corporations, they are the embodiment of centralized power.

Diamond Real Estate

The masters of real estate and finance, controlling the city's housing and commercial infrastructure.
They favor order and stability to maximize their profits. Their economic influence makes them inescapable, even for the government.

Next

The undisputed leaders in transportation and robotics, they manufacture the vehicles and machines that keep the city moving.
Their monopoly on mobility ensures them a central place in Neocron's economy. They strive to push back the limits of technology to remain indispensable.

Tangent Technologies

A militarized corporation that dominates the arms industry and constantly seeks to improve its arsenal.
Their goal is to make Neocron stronger by supplying advanced weapons, even if it means crushing the competition. They see hackers and rebels as a threat to be eliminated.

BioTech Systems

Experts in cybernetic implants and bioengineering, they enhance the human body with their advanced technologies. They work alongside pro-government forces but remain focused on innovation.
Their rivalry with Tangent Technologies is a constant battle for market control.

ProtoPharm

Pharmaceutical and biotech giants, they specialize in medicine and implants.
Their goal is to improve the lives of citizens while maintaining a monopoly on medical care. They oppose the gangs and criminal organizations that threaten public health.

Tsunami Syndicate

A criminal syndicate specializing in entertainment, casinos and nightlife.
They offer forbidden pleasures while fighting for control of the underworld against the Black Dragons. Less brutal but just as dangerous, they rely on seduction and manipulation.

Black Dragon

A crime syndicate that controls the black market, drugs and illegal activities.
They rule the Neocron underworld, competing with other criminal factions. Their influence grows thanks to their underground networks and financial clout.

Brotherhood Of Crahn

An ancient religious sect that masters advanced PSI powers and operates in the shadows.
Once powerful, it seeks to regain its influence and impose its vision of the world. Its members combine spirituality and technology to achieve formidable strength.

Twilight Guardian

Rebel group fighting to overthrow the authoritarian Neocron regime.
Considered terrorists by the government, they carry out attacks to liberate the population. Their base is hidden in Canyon, where they prepare the revolution.

City Mercs

Independent mercenaries who sell their services to the highest bidder, without political allegiance. Based at Military Base, they are made up of former soldiers, bounty hunters and experienced fighters.
Seeking only profit and the honor of combat, they accept contracts for both government and rebels. Their only real enemy is anyone who doesn't pay their debts.
